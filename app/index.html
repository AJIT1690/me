I do design.

I love creating things that help people. I believe content is king. Form follows function. Design should be invisible. Beauty is in simplicity.

With these principles as well as my knowledge of content strategy, information architecture, and brand management, I create designs where the message rings loud and clear.

I do development.

I love building websites and apps. I take mobile-first, responsive design and pair it with the latest technologies—HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript frameworks—to create a fully immersive experience that drives users to action.

Want to get online or into the hands of millions of iOS and Android users? Whether it's a simple blog or a powerful, scalable web and mobile app, I can make it happen.

I do education.

There is little in this world I am more passionate about than education. I'm in love with learning and helping people learn. That's why I'm an Illinois certified teacher for grades 6 through 12 with endorsements in science and mathematics. I provide tutoring and training in a variety of subjects, including ACT/SAT prep, algebra, trigonometry, calculus, chemistry, physics, computer science, technology, and more.
